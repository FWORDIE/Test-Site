Build your future Art School

Introduction 1/25

Imagine you are creating an Art school of the future.

What direction do you believe Art School education should be going in and what choices should be made to steer in that direction?

This survey will ask you a series of questions that you can answer on a sliding scale by agreeing ‘mostly’ or ‘completely’ with a statement. These statements are not necessarily about choosing between two opposing or alternative options, but about considering what you would prioritise. The survey will conclude with reflection questions about your future Art School and the future of GSA more specifically. Each answer will contribute to painting a picture of how you feel Art School education should be changing for the better.

If you want food for thought before you start, you can read 4 fictional scenarios that detail the different directions Art School education could go in.

Why are you asking me to do this?

We are asking you to do this as part of a project called ‘Rethinking GSA: A collective conversation about our future.’ The aim of this project is to understand the priorities of the community of the GSA to inform the GSA’s next steps (these will be formally captured in its next strategic plan).

Unfortunately, this survey only functions on desktop devices. Need a reminder to do it later? Email yourself a link to the survey.
