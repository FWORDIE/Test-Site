Rethinking GSA

Introduction 1/25

Imagine you are creating an Art school of the future.

What direction do you believe Art School education should be going in and what choices should be made to steer in that direction? This survey will ask you a series of questions which you can answer on a sliding scale. Each answer will contribute to painting a picture of how you feel Art School education should be changing for the better.

If you want some food for thought before you start you can read some fictional scenarios which details the different directions Art School education could be going in.

Why are you asking me to do this?

We are asking you to do this as part of a project called ‘Rethinking GSA: A collective conversation about our future.’ The aim of this project is to understand the priorities of the community of the GSA to inform the GSA’s next steps (these will be formally captured in its next strategic plan).
